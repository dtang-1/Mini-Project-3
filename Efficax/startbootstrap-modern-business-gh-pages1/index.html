Innovations in Nanotechnology

Microbots Cleaning Water

Nanobots operate on such a microscopic level that they are able to purify contaminated water. The bots function by using a 3 layer system. The outermost layer is made of graphene oxide it is able to absorb lead from water. The middle layer is made of nickel, by adding nickel the nano bot is able to be controlled by an external magnetic force. The innermost layer is made of platinum. When the compound hydrogen peroxide comes into contact with platinum, the platinum turns the hydrogen peroxide into water and oxygen bubbles propelling the microbot forward. After cleansing water the nanobots can be removed by using a magnetic field, the bots are then purged in an acidic solution to remove all lead contaminants enabling the microbots to be reused.

Nanomachine Made Rockets

A group of physicists recently created the smallest engine in history with just one atom. It does what any other engine does just on an extremely small scale it converts heat into movement. The atom is enveloped in a cone of electromagnetic and microscopic lasers are used to heat the atom to cause it to move back and forward like a piston. There was no particular use for this invention but it shows us how far we have advanced in technology. Imagine if in the future we could create a computer the size of an atom but more powerful than a modern computer today. Though this will be very hard because at a nano scale quantum physics become more complex and harder to understand.
